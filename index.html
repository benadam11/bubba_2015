About Bubba's Bash

Bubba's Bash 2015

Bubba’s Bash is an annual charity event hosted by 8-time PGA Tour winner, 2-time Masters Champion and Pensacola native, Bubba Watson. Motivated by his faith and the desire to give back, Bubba created the event in 2012 as a fun way to support organizations within his communities. Bubba’s Bash continues to grow bigger and better with every year and, for the first time, this year’s event will take place in his hometown of Pensacola, Florida. The local charities benefiting from the event include The Children’s Hospital at Sacred Heart, The YMCA of Northwest Florida and Santa Rosa Kids’ House. These organizations make a positive impact in the lives of countless children and families every day so please join us for an unforgettable evening of music and entertainment.
